About the Raftorium™

Our Raftorium™ offers customizable conditions year-round. Whether you have a lot of experience or you're trying out rafting for the first time, our groundbreaking Raftorium™ technology can offer an experience tailored to any experience you desire!

The Raftorium™ uses powerful pumps to simulate the movement of a fast-flowing river. Adjustable mechanisms beneath the water raise or lower real boulders and large rocks to realistically simulate a natural river.

As water exits the Raftorium™, it goes through specially designed hydroelectic turbines, recycling up to 62% of the energy consumed. All other power is provided by our natural river, allowing the Raftorium™ to be both fun AND clean!

  • Carefully checked and proven to be very safe*
  • Clean and efficient use of green energy allows for affordable prices and low climate impact
  • Realistic river simulation
  • Highly customizable rafting experience for all ages
