Overview

Purpose

I am a pixel artist, and I've worked on the visual assets for Minecraft modifications (one of which has over 9 million downloads). I've noticed a lot of people have shown interest in learning pixel art, so I am making this site to share pixel art, and advice on how to start making it, with the world.

Audience

If you've ever wanted to learn how pixel artists turn little squares into a unique image, you've come to the right place. Whether you want to make your own pixel art, or you appreciate pixel art and want to learn more about it, this will be a good site for you.
